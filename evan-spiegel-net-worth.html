Evan Spiegel | Career

As mentioned before, Evan was born in an affluent household to lawyer parents.

He grew up in Pacific Palisades, California, which is one of the exclusive neighborhoods in the United States.

After graduating high school, he enrolled at Stanford University. He met his future business partners Reggie Brown and Bobby Murphy at the university.

The trio launched the prototype of the app and called it “Picaboo,” which was eventually renamed “Snapchat.”

By 2012, the app had 1 million daily users.

Due to this success, Evan dropped out of the university to focus fully on his business.

After six years, he obtained a B.S in product design from Stanford.
