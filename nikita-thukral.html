Nikita Thukral is an Indian actress and model. She has worked in the South Indian film industry and has been working in Kannada, Telugu, Malayalam, and Tamil films. She was born into a Punjabi Hindu family and was grew up in Mumbai. She completed her Masters in Economics. She was first approached by producer D. Ramanaidu while dining at a hotel in Juhu. Later, he offered her a role in his film titled ‘Hai’ (2002).

Career

From 2002 to 2018, the actress Nikita Thukral acted in more than 35 movies and also acted in 3 television series.

In 2009, the actress Nikita Thukral was also a part of the Aati Rahengi Baharein crew, which was aired on Zee TV.

Then she made her debut in the South film industry with the Telugu film Hai. afterward, she played a role in the movie named ‘Hai’. She has also acted alongside Fahad Fazil in the film named Kaiyethum Doorath.

Nikita Thukral has acted in many Tamil, Malayalam, Kannada, and Telugu movies for which she gained immense popularity and huge honor in her career life.

In 2005, she became famous for appearing in ‘Maharaja’ which was directed by Kitcha Sudeepa. Her performance was undeniable and received positive reviews.

Achievements

In 2008, the actress,  Nikita Thukral has won the ITFA Best Supporting Actress Award for her movie named ‘Saroja’, a Tamil movie.
