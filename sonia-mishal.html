Sonia Mishal is a Pakistan Television Actress and Model. She was born on November 3, 1993, in Karachi, Pakistan. She started her career as a model and became famous in a very short time. After some time of her birth, her family settled in Doha, Qatar. She was raised in Doha city and got her early education from the same city. Her family again relocated to Karachi after some time. Mishal is the youngest and only one sister of three brothers.

Career Journey

She started her career on the television screen when she was selected in an audition as a model, during her studies. Till now, she has appeared in various fashion weeks as a show stopper. She has walked for many famous brands, and fashion designers of Pakistan. She has been featured on the cover of many renowned fashion magazines.

She next then started her career in acting. She made her first acting debut with the Pakistani romantic television serial “Ishqa Waay” along with Sami Khan and Rabab Hashim. The drama was aired on Geo TV in 2015. This drama is the love story of a guy who has two wives where she played the lead role of his second wife Amal. The serial was a huge hit and her performance was acclaimed by the audience.

Since then, she has played many versatile roles in many drama serials, such as she played a lead role of Sania in the 2016 Hum TV drama serial “Khawab Saraye” along with Muneeb Butt, Aiman Khan, Behroze Sabzwari, and Khalid Anam. The drama is the story of a rich man, Abrar Khan’s family.

In 2017, she portrayed the lead role of Umair’s long-termed love and wife, Mariam in the drama serial “Faisla” opposite Ali Abbas This serial is based on a lovely story of a couple. In 2018, she played in a famous drama “Khasara” with Mikaal Zulfikar, Junaid Khan, and Serwat Gilani, which was aired on ARY Digital in 2018. The drama is concerned about four friends who get intertwined into their relationship due to an affair of money in the name of love. This drama was critically acclaimed and admired by the audience due to its storyline. Her upcoming drama will be “GT Road”, starring Inayat Khan, Kashif Mehmood, and Maria Malik which will be aired on A-Plus TV.
