In The Elder Scrolls video games, each adventure starts with the player character escaping prison. In our world, each adventure begins with a fun daily word challenge game called Wordle.

Continuing to sweep the globe like an enchanted broom, Wordle seems here to stay. With that comes the often vexatious challenge associated with guessing the word within the allotted six guesses. It’s all about maintaining win streaks; you don’t want to miss out on bragging rights.

When the challenge gets tough, there’s no dishonor in requiring a few gentle tips and tricks.
