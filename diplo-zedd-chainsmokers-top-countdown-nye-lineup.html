Diplo, Zedd And The Chainsmokers Top Countdown NYE Lineup

New Year’s Eve is quickly closing in on us as 2016 draws to an end, and already we’ve seen a number of enticing festival lineups to ring in 2017. Lights All Nights recently unveiled their full lineup for the end of the year holiday, with big names like deadmau5, Nero and Tchami gracing the roster. Now, Insomaniac’s Countdown NYE has dropped their full list of performers for the festival’s inaugural two day edition.

Insomniac have really outdone themselves with this one, securing a number of the biggest DJs in the game as performers. Diplo, Zedd, The Chainsmokers, Steve Aoki, Alesso, and Zomboy serve as the roster’s star power, but there’s a little of everything for festival goers to enjoy as well. Bass music is represented with acts like Kill The Noise, NGHTMRE, Rezz, Baauer, RL Grime and Virtual Riot, while artists like Seven Lions, Showtek, Deorro, A-Trak, Ghastly and Grandtheft offer up plenty of diversity.

With a staggering number of EDM’s most celebrated acts comprising most of the lineup, Countdown NYE is shaping up to be one of the biggest end of the year parties around. The event will run December 30 and 31 at the NOS Events Center in Southern California, and you can purchase tickets here.
