Presenter Kate Humble is well known for her roles on A Country Life for Half the Price with Kate Humble, Springwatch, and her several books.

Something fans may not know about Kate is her relationship with Ludo Graham, to whom she is married.

The couple have been married since 1992, and live on a working farm together in Wye Valley.

View Instagram Post

Are Kate Humble and Ludo Graham married?

Yes, Kate Humble and Ludo Graham have been married since 1992, when Kate was 23 and Ludo was 31.

On Kate’s website she writes: “On her return to the UK Kate got her first job in television as a runner and met Producer/Director Ludo Graham who she married in 1992.”

Speaking about their life together, Kate’s site says:

“In 2007 Kate and Ludo moved to a smallholding in Wales and in 2011 set up ‘Humble by Nature’ a rural skills school on a working farm in the Wye Valley. They live with a variety of feathered and furry livestock and three dogs.”

Do Kate Humble and Ludo Graham have children?

Kate Humble and Ludo Graham do not have any kids together. Kate has been open about the fact she does not want children and said:

“I just knew having children wasn’t for me. Luckily I married someone who agreed.”

On Lorraine in 2017, Kate spoke about her decision not to have kids, and stated:

“By choice, I have said I do not want to have children. And that still seems to be quite odd, and you do still seem to be quite judged for it.”
