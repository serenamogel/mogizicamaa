Maya Varsano: Gal Gadots 2nd Daughter Everything to Know

March 04, 2024
blog

Have you ever wondered about the life of Maya Varsano, the second daughter of the renowned actress Gal Gadot and her husband, Yaron Varsano? Gal Gadot was celebrated for portraying DC’s iconic Wonder Woman, and Yaron, a real estate developer and hotelier, welcomed Maya into their family. Let’s delve into the intriguing story of this charming family.

Maya Varsano’s Pre-Fame Beginnings

Maya Varsano was thrust into the limelight even before her birth. This is because her mother, Gal Gadot, is a famous actress and held the title of Miss Israel in 2004.

The Arrival of Maya

Maya’s journey began on March 20, 2017, when she was born in Los Angeles. Gal Gadot joyfully shared the news on her Instagram, expressing her gratitude for the new addition to their family.

A Surprise Announcement

Gal Gadot’s second pregnancy was a well-kept secret until she finished filming for the 2017 Wonder Woman film. She surprised the world with the revelation in November 2016 through a heartfelt Instagram post with a captivating black-and-white photo revealing her growing baby bump.

Maya’s Silver Screen Debut

Maya Varsano technically debuted in the famous DC comic movie ‘Wonder Woman.’ The catch? Gal Gadot was pregnant with her during the filming in 2016.

Gal Gadot’s Clever Concealment

Gal Gadot’s pregnancy was skillfully concealed from the production team and her co-stars. In an interview with Marie Claire, she confessed to hiding her pregnancy to avoid special treatment on set. However, when she returned for reshoots, her baby bump was visible, necessitating CGI to remove it from the screen.

A Decade of Love

Maya Varsano’s parents, Gal Gadot and Yaron Varsano, have been happily married since 2008. Their love story began when they met through mutual friends at a yoga retreat a few years before tying the knot. Despite their ten-year age gap, they quickly became close friends.

The Proposal and Wedding

Yaron Varsano proposed to Gal Gadot with a stunning round-cut diamond set on a platinum band just months before their 2008 wedding. Their intimate wedding ceremony occurred in Tel Aviv, Israel, with Gal in a fitted lace gown and Yaron in a classic black suit.

Meet Aunt Dana Gadot

Maya Varsano has a maternal aunt, Dana Gadot. Unlike her famous sister Gal, Dana is a psychology student at the Interdisciplinary Center Herzliya in Herzliya, Israel.

Sisterly Bond

Maya is not an only child; she has an older sister named Alma Varsano, who is six years older. The sisters share a strong bond and often accompany their parents on vacations and outings. Gal frequently shares heartwarming pictures of their family moments.

Maya Varsano’s journey is undoubtedly filled with love, joy, and abundant family moments. With her talented parents and a loving older sister, Maya’s future looks as bright as her mother’s iconic Wonder Woman persona.
