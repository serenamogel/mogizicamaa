Post Malone Net Worth – $50 million

What is Post Malone’s net worth? Who is Post Malone’s fiancée?

Introduction

Post Malone is an American singer, rapper, and record producer best known for his debut single “White Iverson.”

Biography

He was born on July 4, 1995, in Syracuse, New York, USA, as Austin Richard Post.

He has revealed that he’s partially Italian and Native American.

His father, Rich Post, worked as a disc jockey for CNY weddings with Whirlin’ Disc DJs. 

”My dad was always playing music,” the award-winning singer said in an interview. ”Not like playing music, but listening to music. And he put me on everything… Folk and funk and rap.”

At age 9, Malone moved to Grapevine, Texas, with his father and stepmother, Jodie.

Post worked at a Chicken Express as a teenager.

He enrolled in Tarrant County College but dropped out.

White Iverson

He first made a splash with his 2015 track “White Iverson” on Soundcloud, where it became a hit after receiving praise from Mac Miller and Wiz Khalifa.

”White Iverson” reached #14 on the Billboard Hot 100 songs chart in the US. The song sold more than five million digital copies.

In August 2015, Post inked a recording contract with Republic Records.

Debut Album

© Getty Images

In December 2016, Malone released his debut studio album, Stoney. It debuted at #six on the Billboard 200.

”Stoney” features guest appearances from Quavo, Kehlani, and Justin Bieber. It sold over 3 million copies.

His second album, ”Beerbongs & Bentleys,” was released on April 27, 2018. It was certified platinum in just four days.

”Beerbongs & Bentleys” broke streaming records on Spotify, having gained 47.9 million streams in the United States on the first day of its release.

The album features guest appearances from YG, G-Eazy, Nicki Minaj, Ty Dolla Sign, 21 Savage, and Swae Lee.

The album’s lead single, “Rockstar,” featuring 21 Savage, reached #1 on the US Billboard Hot 100.

In August 2018, Malone’s plane was forced to make an emergency landing after two tires blew out when it took off from New Jersey’s Teterboro Airport. 

”I’m terrified of flying in the first place, so every time I get on a flight, I always try my best to fall asleep,” Post later confessed. ”There was a sensor malfunction upfront, so we had to turn around and land again.”

His third album, ”Hollywood’s Bleeding,” was released in September 2019. It sold more than 489,000 in the first week of its release.

The album features guest appearances from Young Thug, Swae Lee, DaBaby, SZA, Future, Travis Scott, Ozzy Osbourne, Lil Baby, Meek Mill, and Halsey.

In 2021, Malone performed at a virtual concert for the 25th Anniversary of the Pokémon celebration.

Post Malone – Net Worth

© Getty Images

So, how much is Post Malone worth?

Malone earned most of his wealth from selling copies of his albums (Hollywood’s Bleeding, Beerbongs & Bentleys, and Stoney). Post grosses around $500,000 per concert.

Malone has appeared in Super Bowl commercials.

In January 2020, he starred in a Doritos commercial for their Flamin’ Hot Limon flavored chips. Post has partnered with the American beer brand Bud Light.

He also sells merch on shop.postmalone.com. His YouTube channel has over 13 billion views, meaning about $39 million in revenue before taxes.

In 2020, Post launched his own line of French rosé wine, Maison No. 9.

In 2019, Malone bought a 5-bed, 6-bath mansion for $3 million. It sits on 6.75 acres in a super secluded area outside Salt Lake City.

The property amenities include a pool, gourmet chef’s kitchen, home bar, home gym, wine cellar, basketball court, and a fire pit.

”I kinda just wanted to get away where I can have my own oasis,” Malone said. ”We’re building a studio out there right now.”

In September 2018, Post’s former home was broken into and burgled of $20k in items.

”I’m trying to get all the money I can, not so I can stop making music, but so if I needed to, I’d be good,” Malone told Paper Mag in 2017. He went on to say: ”This world is fu####d in every aspect, but you gotta make it work.”
