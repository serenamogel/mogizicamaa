Tara Hastings Family Background

Meteorologist Tara Hastings was born in Hammond, Indiana, near Lake Michigan. She grew up in her hometown and attended Valparaiso University, where she earned a degree in meteorology.

Sadly, her Father was diagnosed with Alzheimer’s and had early on-set. Besides her family and career, Tara also takes care of her dad.

Hastings participated in the Valparaiso University Storm Intercept Team’s 10-day summer storm-chasing mission. She has the AMS mark of approval and is an American Meteorological Society member.

Before arriving in Indianapolis, Tara worked at WDTN in Dayton, Ohio, the same station where Randy Ollis had his first job. Returning to Indiana and being nearer to her family and friends excites her.
