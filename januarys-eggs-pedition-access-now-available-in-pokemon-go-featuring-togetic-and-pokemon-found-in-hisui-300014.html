January's Eggs-pedition Access now available in Pokmon GO, featuring Togetic and Pokmon found in

blog
Starting today, January 1st, and running through to January 29th, at 8:00pm local time, Trainers in Pokémon GO can purchase a ticket for Eggs-pedition Access to gain access to Timed Research and special bonuses. After purchasing the ticket, Trainers will have until January 31st, at 8:00pm local time to complete all research tasks and claim their rewards.

The Eggs-pedition Access: January ticket can be purchased for US$5.00 (or the equivalent pricing tier in each local currency) from the in-game shop. This Ticket can also be gifted to any friend with a Friendship level of Great Friends or higher. Eggs-pedition Access Tickets will be available every month throughout the Pokémon GO: Timeless Travels season.

Eggs-pedition Access: January bonuses
The following bonuses will be available to Trainers throughout January from when they purchase the ticket, until January 31st, at 8:00pm local time:

  • Trainers will receive one single-use Incubator for the first PokéStop or Gym spin of the day
  • 3× XP awarded for the first PokéStop or Gym spin of the day
  • 3× XP awarded for the first catch of the day
  • Trainers will be able to open up to 50 Gifts per day
  • Trainers can receive up to 150 Gifts per day from spinning PokéStop and Gym Photo Discs
  • Trainers will be able to hold up to 40 Gifts in the Item Bag
Eggs-pedition Access: January Timed Research
This Timed Research offers Trainers opportunities to earn XP, Stardust, and encounters with Pokémon found in the Hisui region, including Gible, Togetic, and Hisuian Growlithe. Trainers who complete all the Timed Research tasks associated with this ticket will receive XP, Stardust, and an encounter with Togetic.
