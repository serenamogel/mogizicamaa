Who is Shel Rasten?

Shel Rasten is an American actor and producer. He is known for his popular characters in The Boulevard (2013), FAMOUS (and on the List) (2010), CSI: Miami (2002), and more.

His latest project was a TV movie titled Sharknado 5: Global Swarming which was released in 2017.

Who are the parents of Shel Rasten?

His birthdate is 31 March 1982 and he was born in the United States. The name of his mother is Charo (singer) but his father was Kjell Rasten (producer).

Shel’s ancestry is Caucasian and as per his academics, he is trained at Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.

Shel Rasten- Professional Life, Career

Shel Rasten started his acting career in 2006 by appearing in an episode of CSI: Miami as Steve Dixon. He also briefly did two TV movies titled Bird Dog (2011) and Sharknado 5: Global Swarming (2017).

Moreover, he was seen in the 2007’s movie Detention as Jack. But his popularity is mostly with TV series. He has appeared in short roles but was appreciated a lot for his acting. His notable roles are in,

  • Connected (2007) as Troy floret

  • Anger Management (2013) as Rocker Dude
  • Workaholics (2014) as Frank
  • Parks and Recreation (2014) as Xander
  • Baskets (2016) as Farm Boy

Also, Shel as a producer has produced two short projects The Boulevard (2013) plus FAMOUS (2010).
