Shop Ben Affleck's Sustainable Rothy's Sneakers 2024

Some celebrities are an ever-reliable source of style inspiration (hello, Ryan Gosling), and some are dark horses who love to offer the occasional surprise. Ben Affleck is one of the latter. Step aside, J.Lo—it's your husband's sneakers that are taking center stage today.

We—along with Pedro Pascal—have always been a fan of Rothy's. From the brand's commitment to sustainability to the thoughtful craftsmanship, you really can't go wrong with a pair of these straightforward kicks. Can't decide which ones to get? This is where Affleck comes in.

Take a page from the actor's book and opt for the RS01 sneakers. A flexible upper, contoured footbed and padded ankle collar create the premium comfort that the brand is known for. The color blocking and gum sole give off a retro feel, bringing vintage vibes to any outfit. And don't worry about getting them dirty and scuffed; just throw them in the wash and they're as good as new. The best part is that they are impressively sustainable. Knit from about 28 plastic water bottles, these are shoes that you can shop with a clean conscience. And if our word and Affleck's endorsement aren't enough, more than 1,000 customers can vouch for the quality and comfort.

Choose from 17 different colorways to find your next everyday pair of sneakers. Whether you want to wear them to the office or on your next trip, these guys can go everywhere. Now go. Better get your hands on them before Batman comes for you.
