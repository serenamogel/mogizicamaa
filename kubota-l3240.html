Kubota L3240 Compact Utility Tractor is fitted with a Kubota diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 100.5 ci [1.6 L] engine, which delivers 34 hp (25.4 kW) power at a rated rpm 2700.

Kubota L3240 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 1.6L 3-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD, hydrostatic power steering, wet disc brakes, 3,750 lbs [1701 kg] Rear lift (at ends), and 3153 to 3748 pounds weight.
